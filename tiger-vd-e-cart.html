Tiger - VD

Tiger VD & VD+, model E-Carts are designed with an efficient front fascia that combines electric motors with a drivetrain and battery system. It is a closed-body loading container with a closed driver Cabin, making them ideal for applications such as small merchants delivering grocery items, various industrial applications, agricultural applications, courier services, vegetables and fruit sales in the streets, and hotel and canteen food supplies to the nearest branches and customers. It is classified as an L3 vehicle and is registered at any Regional Transport Office (RTO) in India, same like ICE-powered diesel vehicles
